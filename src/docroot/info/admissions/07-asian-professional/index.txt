**Overview**

The Graduate School of Law, in collaboration with the Asian Satellite Campuses Institute is offering a special research-based doctoral program which primarily targets professionals in government positions, not-for-profit groups, professional societies, and academic institutions from select Asian countries. The program is designed for students who have already completed a masterâ€™s degree and who wish to earn a doctorate degree. The program is unique in that it provides enrolled students with an opportunity to deepen their knowledge and experience and earn a doctorate degree without having to suspend their career. 

Upon completion of coursework, submission of dissertation, and successful oral defense, this program leads to the Nagoya University degree of LL.D. in Comparative Law and Political Science.

For further details of the program, please visit the following website: 
[the project website](http://asci.nagoya-u.ac.jp)

**Special Admissions Round Information (Updated 1 October 2015)**

To mark the opening of Satellite Campus facilities in Uzbekistan, Laos and Myanmar, the Graduate School of Law is running a special admissions round for eligible applicants of these three countries.  

**Key information**

- Successful applicants will be expected to commence the program in April 2016.
- Application documentation is available at the top right hand corner of this page
- The deadline for submitting the application is 18 December 2015.
- Please submit your application directly to your local Satellite Campus office. 
- Your local representative will liaise directly with Nagoya University main campus on your behalf and will be able to respond to questions associated with admissions.
- Contact details of your local Satellite Campus Office are provided on the last page of the admissions documentation.
