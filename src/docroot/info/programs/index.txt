The Graduate School of Law offers several full postgraduate degree
programs in both English and in Japanese, at both the Masters and the
Doctoral levels.  For detailed information, please see the relevant
links under the **Postgraduate** and **Undergraduate** headings to the
right.

Information on Exchange arrangements with other academic institutions,
including the Campus Asia initiative of the Faculty of Law and the
NUPACE program covering the entire University, see the **Exchange
Programs** links to the right.

Internships and special short-term exchange programs with individual
universities are also offered by the Faculty. See the relevant links
to the right for further information.
