There is a single alumni association for all GSL
graduates.  To offer or receive notices of events,
or if you have a proposal for enhancement of alumni
services, please [contact the Association secretary][alumni].

[alumni]: /support/index.html#alumni_association

If you wish to contact a former classmate
or tie up with a circle of GSL graduates, 
the [International Student Advisor][advisor]
may be able to help with contact details.

[advisor]: /support/index.html#international_student_advisor

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA) maintains a
useful [database of local Japan alumni associations][mof-study].

[mof-study]: /support/index.html#study_in_japan_website
